User Research

In-depth understanding of the core factors that influence a user experience both psychologically and sociologically: useful, usable, findable, credible, desirable, accessible, and valuable. Performing a mix of user research methodologies: usability testing, user interviews, card sorting, tree testing, first-click testing, A/B testing, focus groups, and surveys; that support or negate creative solutions using the collected qualitative and quantitative data.

User Interface Design

Comprehensive understanding of design principles with professional experience and an earned degree in digital media and graphic design. As a member of the Toastmasters International Club, the continual growth in communication, leadership skills, and public speaking help cultivate a strong balanced relationship between fulfilling the needs of the business and the needs of the user.

Problem-solving

Using both critical thinking and logical reasoning in the design thinking process to understand the complexities of problems not yet clearly defined by focusing on human needs and behaviors from different points of view. Having professional experience and certified training in problem-solving: imperative in the development of user research and its resolve, user interface design.
